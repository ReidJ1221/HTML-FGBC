About Fighting Game Bootcamp

History

Since there are multiple wayts of practicing for sports, we would like to be that for eSports and namely fighting games.
For those that don't know, Fighting Games(FGs) are a genre of player vs player(pvp) games where they control character and the player to finish a match with more HP than the opponent wins.
FGs were popular during the arcade era in the 80 and 90s and since fizzled out due to a "high barrier" of entry compared to other competitive genres and a lack of resources for players to grow.

Arcades by EVO created in 1994.

Nowadays with the internet, connecting with others has never been easier and that's where we come in.

What we are about?
